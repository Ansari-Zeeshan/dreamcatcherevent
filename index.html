About Us

Who we Are?

Dreamcatcher events is a full-service events & exhibition company dedicated to quality and brilliance. We focus on custom made solutions for every project, We attempt towards the immaculate execution of all events that come to us. Our services are designed to help you stage your event from conception to theme generation to creation of experiences

Our Vision

It's not just about your guests, it's also about you. Our aim is to make the pre-event design, the planning process, the post event debrief and the reporting as stress free for you as possible.
